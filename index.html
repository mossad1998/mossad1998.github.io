About Me

Embedded Software Engineer

I’m a Mechatronics Engineer (Class of 2021) specializing in embedded systems, real-time control, and robotics. I design and ship firmware that talks to the physical world—sensors, actuators, and everything in between. My work spans automotive ECUs, motion control, power electronics, and vision-enabled systems, with a focus on reliability, performance, and clean, testable code. I’m obsessed with turning data into decisions and ideas into hardware that moves.

Technical Experience

R&D Software Engineer — Valeo

Work with top-tier OEMs on production ECUs across multiple AUTOSAR Classic components and embedded stacks. Responsibilities include design, integration, and validation of:
  • Security & Boot: Secure boot and secure flash bootloader (XCP/UDS), crypto services (CSM/Crypto), key handling, seed-key, and firmware authenticity/integrity aligned with ISO 21434.
  • Diagnostics: UDS (ISO 14229) over CAN/FlexRay/DoIP; DCM, DEM, NvM integration; session/control/security access; DIDs/routines; tester scripting and trace analysis.
  • OS & Scheduling: MICROSAR OS (AUTOSAR Classic) task/config generation; event/alarm scheduling; ISR design; timing analysis. Experience with FreeRTOS where non-AUTOSAR RTOS is required.
  • BSW/MCAL: CAN/LIN/FlexRay stacks, E2E protection, SecOC (where applicable), COM/PduR, MemIf/Fee/Fls, Wdg, Det, and low-level drivers.
  • Flashing & Update: In-plant and field flashing flows (bootloader + application), recovery strategies, and robustness (power-loss safe, dual-bank).
  • Quality & Safety: Requirements traceability, unit & integration tests, ISO 26262 compliant workflows, code reviews, and coverage.
Tooling & validation: Vector CANoe/CANape, CAPL, Diagnostic Console, UDS testers, Trace32, oscilloscopes/logic analyzers, SIL/HIL benches, CI pipelines. Deliverables include ARXML configs, BSW integration, and customer-specific adaptations meeting OEM coding standards and timing/CPU/RAM targets.

Software Team Lead — ASmarine

Led a 5-engineer software/embedded team for RoboSub 2021. Delivered the vehicle’s electrical “heart” and hardware–software interface (sensors, ESCs, servos, compute). Ran a 3-month workshop on communication protocols, embedded interfaces, and electronics fundamentals.

Embedded Software Engineer — ASU Racing Team

Co-built a competition-grade ROV for MATE ROV 2020. Designed a serial communication board linking electrical subsystems with the main computer. Implemented embedded modules: BLDC control, IMU integration, voltage/current monitoring, and servo control.
